Peanut Punch

Ingredients:

  • 2/3 cup peanut butter
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 2 dashes of bitters
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp granola (for topping)
  • 1 cup ice

Directions:

  1. Add ingredients into a blender making sure to put the ice and milk in first.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Allow to cool then strain and transfer to a mug or bottle.
  4. Top with granola.
