Dhalpuri Roti

Ingredients for Filling:

  • 5 to 6 cups of water
  • 8 oz split peas
  • 2 tsp tumeric powder
  • 1 ½ tbsp salt
  • 12 leaves/ ¼ cup chadon beni/culantro
  • 1 tbsp scotch bonnet pepper (seeds & core removed)
  • 3 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp roasted geera

Ingredients for Dough:

  • 4 cups all purpose flour (sifted)
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 1 tsp + ½ cup melted ghee

Directions:

  1. Add water to a pot and bring to a boil over medium heat.
  2. Add split peas, turmeric & salt.
  3. Partly cover the pot and allow to cook for 30 minutes or until tender.
  4. For the dough, add flour, baking powder, brown sugar, and salt to a bowl.
  5. Mix before making a hole in the middle.
  6. Add water and knead for 3 to 5 minutes.
  7. Once dough is formed, place in a bowl, rub tsp of ghee all over it, cover with clingwrap or a damp cloth and allow to rest for 30 minutes.
  8. Once the peas are cooked, drain off excess liquid and transfer to a bowl.
  9. Add Chadon Beni, pepper, garlic & roasted geera.
  10. Using your mill or food processor, grind your dhal to a smooth consistency and set aside.
  11. Divide dough into 6 equal pieces; cover with a damp cloth and allow to rest for 10 minutes.
  12. Using your hand, press each one out into a 4” disc, ensuring not to press out the middle of the dough.
  13. Fill each disc with 1/4 cup or more of filling.
  14. Seal each one by bringing the ends and pinching them together.
  15. Once sealed, dust with flour, place on a floured surface, cover with a damp cloth and allow to rest for 30 minutes.
  16. On a lightly floured surface, using your rolling pin, roll each loya out to a 12” disc.
  17. Heat your tawa over high heat.
  18. Once heated, brush with ghee.
  19. Remove excess flour from your roti by gently tossing it from hand to hand a few times.
  20. Immediately place on tawa and spread your roti out to a circle.
  21. Brush with ghee and flip after 30 seconds.
  22. Once flipped, apply ghee to any dry spots and allow to cook for 30 seconds.
  23. Once cooked, fold roti, remove from tawa and place in an insulated container and allow to sit for 5 to 10 minutes before enjoying!
