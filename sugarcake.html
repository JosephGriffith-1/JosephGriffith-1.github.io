Sugar Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups finely grated coconut (with brown husk removed)
  • 4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 1/2 tsp almond essence
  • Few drops of food coloring (optional)

Directions:

  1. Line 2 baking trays with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Place all ingredients into an iron pot.
  3. Bring mixture to a boil on medium heat, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon.
  4. Stir until the mixture thickens and the sugar starts crystallizing on the sides of the pot.
  5. Quickly turn off the heat and begin scooping bits of the mixture on to the two baking trays lined with parchment paper.
  6. Leave to cool completely before enjoying!
