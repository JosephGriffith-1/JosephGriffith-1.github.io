Curry Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 3 tbsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste (minced or grated ginger)
  • 4 tbsp garlic paste (minced or grated garlic)
  • 2 whole garlic cloves
  • 1 medium onion chopped
  • 1 tsp whole geera or cumin
  • 6 shadon beni leaves finely chopped
  • 8 carapulay (curry) leaves
  • 4 pimentos finely chopped
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 2 lbs mixed chicken parts
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil

Directions:

  1. Toast geera seeds in a frying pan for 2 to 3 minutes then grind to a powder in a spice grinder or mortar and pestle.
  2. Add oil to a pan then add whole garlic cloves. Allow to fry (chunkay) for 2 minutes.
  3. Add garlic paste, ginger paste, turmeric, chopped onion, pimento, carapulay leaves and ground spices to the pot then saut√© for 3 minutes.
  4. Add half cup of water and 1/3 of the shadon beni to the pot.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper then allow to simmer until a thick paste is formed.
  6. Add chicken, scotch bonnet pepper, and 1/3 of the shadon beni to the pot. Cover and allow to simmer on medium/low heat for 10 minutes.
  7. Add one cup of water to chicken then bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover pot and allow to simmer for another 10 minutes.
  8. Turn off flame then add remaining shadon beni.
  9. Serve with your favourite side dish.
