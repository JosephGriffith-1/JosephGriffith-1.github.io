Grapefruit Spritzer

Ingredients for Juice:

  • 4 oz freshly squeezed grapefruit juice
  • 1 oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 5-6 dashes Angostura Orange Bitters

Ingredients for Ginger & Rosemary simple syrup:

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 stalk rosemary

Directions:

  1. Combine juice ingredients then set aside.
  2. Add sugar and water to a pan or pot then simmer on medium/low heat until it reaches boiling point and sugar is dissolved.
  3. Add ginger and rosemary then allow to steep and cool to room temperature.
  4. Add ice, 1 oz simple syrup & 4 oz juice to a glass.
  5. Top up glass with club soda & mix to combine.
