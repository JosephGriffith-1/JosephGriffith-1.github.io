Ingredients:

Frybake

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • 1 ½ tsp Baking powder
  • 1 ½ tbsp Granulated sugar
  • 3 tbsp Butter
  • ¾ cup Water
  • 3 cups cooking oil – For frying

Saltfish Buljol

  • 1 Pack Boneless salted fish
  • 2 tbsp Soya-bean Oil
  • 5 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 1 medium Red onion (small diced)
  • 3 Medium Tomatoes (medium diced)
  • 1 medium Sweet pepper (small diced)
  • 1 Chive (thinly slices)
  • 1 sprig Parsley (finely chopped)
  • 1 Lime
  • Black Pepper- to taste

Directions:

  1. Boil the saltfish as desired and cut all vegetables as mentioned.
  2. In a bowl, add all the dry ingredients for the bake and mix well. Add a little water at a time kneading as you go.
  3. Knead the dough for about 8-10 minutes until a ball is formed.
  4. Cover with a damp paper towel and tea towel. Set aside to rest in a warm environment for 25 mins.
  5. Take the dough and separate it into equal sized balls by rolling and pinching the dough; Allow dough to rest for another 5 mins.
  6. In a pot, heat oil to 325F and add bakes one at time. Once the bottom has browned, turn over and allow the next side to get browned as well. Place bakes into a container lined with paper towel or brown bags.
  7. In a pan, sauté all the vegetables over medium heat for 3 minutes.
  8. Add saltfish and take off the fire. Mix in fresh parsley, chive and lime juice. Any additional seasonings can be added at this point.
  9. Enjoy!
